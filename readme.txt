 Gather email addresses of your blog's readers and automagically email your blog posts to them.

== Description ==
This plugin adds a widget you can use in your theme to complete integrating the Blogsend.io service. It will gather emails for you and automatically send your new blog posts out to your captive audience!

Subscription at Blogsend.io required to work.

== Installation ==
1. In your WordPress admin panel, go to Plugins > New Plugin, search for Blogsend and click "Install now"
2. Or, download the plugin and upload the contents of `blogsend.zip` to your plugins directory, which usually is `/wp-content/plugins/`
3. Activate the "Blogsend.io" plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress
4. Under the "Appearance" menu, go to "Customize", then under "Widgets" push the "Add a widget" button
5. Search for and click the widget called "Blogsend.io", and position it where you want to in your theme
6. From your [Blogsend.io](https://blogsend.io) dashboard, grab your API key, and paste it into the "Blogsend.io" widget's options panel
